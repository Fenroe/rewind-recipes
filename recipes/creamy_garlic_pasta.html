Creamy garlic pasta

Description

My go-to recipe for pasta. It's quick, easy and tastes incredible.

Ingredients

  • Pasta of your choosing (I prefer penne)
  • Garlic
  • Garlic powder
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Heavy cream
  • Oregano
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Fill a saucepan halfway with water and set it over high heat. When it reaches a simmer, salt the water generously and add a drizzle of olive oil. Then, add pasta and cook according to package instructions.
  2. Drain the pasta, reserving some of the water it was cooked in. Add pasta to a preheated pan, alongside crushed garlic and olive oil.
  3. Add reserved pasta water, heavy cream and cheese. Season with garlic powder and oregano, and reduce the heat. Serve once the sauce has thickened and coats the pasta.
