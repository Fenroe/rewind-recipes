Black bean curry

Description

One of my favourite vegetarian dishes. Cheap, easy and very nutritious

Ingredients

  • Crushed tomatoes
  • Tomato paste
  • Black beans
  • Onion
  • Coriander
  • Cumin
  • Coconut milk

Steps

  1. Add diced onions to a heavy-bottom pan over medium heat. Cook until translucent.
  2. Mix tomato paste in with the onions, and add the crushed tomatoes and coocnut milk. Mix well and bring to a simmer. Add coriander and cumin and stir. Leave covered for about ten to fifteen minutes.
  3. Add black beans and simmer uncovered for a further ten minutes. Serve over rice and with bread.
